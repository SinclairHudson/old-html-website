Experience:

Starterhacks 2019

Starterhacks is canada's biggest beginner-oriented hackathon, hosted at Waterloo. I attended Starterhacks 2019 as a mentor. This meant that I would run around the event, helping people with their projects, usually when they ran into errors. I really enjoyed being a part of many different, interesting projects that people were working on. Overall, it was a lot less stressful than actually competing.

What I Learned:

  • Helping debug code that wasn't written by me.
  • How to articulate why certain code didn't work as intended, and how to fix it.
  • How to effectively teach good debugging and documentation practices.
